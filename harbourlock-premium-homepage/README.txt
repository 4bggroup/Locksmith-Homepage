HARBOURLOCK PREMIUM LOCKSMITH HOMEPAGE
=====================================

Open index.html in a browser, or upload the whole folder to a web server.
Do not move index.html away from the assets folder unless you also update the paths.

Folder structure
----------------
index.html
assets/css/style.css
assets/js/app.js
assets/images/*

Included
--------
- Premium Australian locksmith branding and unique website imagery
- Responsive desktop, tablet and mobile layouts
- Opening lock animation
- Emergency rescue console
- Postcode coverage demonstration
- Four-step booking form
- Photo/video upload interface
- Scheduled and emergency attendance paths
- Indicative pricing tool
- Commercial access-control content
- Service-area checker
- FAQ accordion and FAQ schema
- LocalBusiness/Locksmith schema
- Mobile call and booking actions

Before publishing
-----------------
Replace the demonstration business number, email, URL, locations, licensing,
service claims, pricing, reviews and structured-data details with verified data.
The booking form is a front-end demonstration. Connect it to WordPress,
ServiceM8, Tradify, Jobber, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Formspree or a custom API.
